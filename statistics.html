Problem Statement
Cancer is a disease that has plagued the world and we are trying to rid of the one that is the most important being breast cancer. This has led to us to using this dataset being a breast cancer dataset that gets measurements of the chest and classifies them into having malginant and benign tumors.


We look to use machine learning, particularly OpenCV to try and use images as the input for our dataset and predicitons. This can work as an application for those who are looking to find whether they have cancer or not and are too shy as breasts can be a sensitive topic where a computer can be used to help reduce any human involvement.
